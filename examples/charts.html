Charts

Two distinct vocabularies. Don't mix them. Consumer charts are warm, single-series, calm. Clinical charts use a defined data palette and never appear in chrome / actions.

Consumer — sparkline (Women app)

--chart-consumer-stroke · --chart-consumer-area

Clinical — multi-series radar (Professionals product)

Series A — communication Series B — restricted patterns

--chart-clinical-seriesA · --chart-clinical-seriesB · …seriesC, D, E available

Bar (consumer) — weekly summary
